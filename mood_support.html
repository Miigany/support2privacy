2. 常见问题

记录内容不见了怎么办？

  • 请确认是否删除并重新安装过 App。删除 App 可能会同时删除本地数据。
  • 请确认是否更换了设备，或是否通过系统备份恢复了 App 数据。
  • 如果问题持续，请尽量提供发生时间、设备型号和操作步骤，方便排查。

提醒没有按时出现怎么办？

  • 请在 iOS 设置中确认 Mellow Cloud 的通知权限已开启。
  • 请检查专注模式、勿扰模式或系统通知摘要是否影响提醒显示。
  • 请确认你在 App 内设置了正确的提醒时间。

我可以同步或备份数据吗？

当前版本以本地存储为主，不提供开发者服务器云同步。若你启用了 Apple 提供的设备级备份，相关备份能力由系统服务提供方处理。

2. Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if my records are missing?

  • Check whether the App was deleted and reinstalled. Deleting the App may also delete local data.
  • Check whether you changed devices or restored app data from a system backup.
  • If the issue continues, please share the approximate time, device model, and steps that led to the issue.

What should I do if reminders do not appear on time?

  • Make sure notifications for Mellow Cloud are enabled in iOS Settings.
  • Check whether Focus mode, Do Not Disturb, or scheduled notification summary affects reminder delivery.
  • Confirm that the reminder time is set correctly in the App.

Can I sync or back up my data?

The current version is local-first and does not provide developer-operated server cloud sync. If you enable device-level backup provided by Apple, that backup is handled by the system service provider.
